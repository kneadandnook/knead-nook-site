Privacy Policy

We only collect the information needed to help with orders, inquiries, and customer support. We keep it simple, respectful, and focused on serving our customers well.

What we collect

When you place an order or submit a custom inquiry, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, requested date, order details, delivery information, and any notes you choose to provide.

How we use it

  • To review and fulfill your order
  • To respond to custom order requests and general inquiries
  • To contact you about pickup, delivery, timing, or order questions
  • To provide customer support when needed

How we share information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only use the information you provide to run our bakery services and communicate with you about your order or inquiry.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information submitted through our website, but no website or transmission method can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.
