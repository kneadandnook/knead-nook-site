Allergen Notice

We want customers to have clear information before ordering. Please review this notice carefully if you or someone you are ordering for has food allergies or sensitivities.

Our kitchen handles common allergens, and we cannot guarantee that any item is completely free from cross-contact.

Common allergens in our kitchen

  • Wheat
  • Dairy
  • Eggs
  • Tree nuts and peanuts
  • Soy

Cross-contact

Because our products are prepared in a shared kitchen environment, there is always a risk of cross-contact between ingredients, even when a recipe does not intentionally include a specific allergen.

Please tell us

If you have an allergy or dietary concern, include it in your order notes or custom inquiry so we can let you know what may or may not be possible.
